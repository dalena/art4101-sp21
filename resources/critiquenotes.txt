Helpful questions for critique
- What kind of changes would you make to your work if you could?
- Asking about the complexity of the work. What was the artist thinking when creating the piece and how it may affect them after they made the piece. Why certain variables were chosen
-What method did you use and what technical aspects did you use to achieve x effect?
-Having more people talk about your work


-Art direction/unborn sibling




Not helpful questions/statements
-"I Like" opinions (What are the aspects that are working that make you like it?)
-Self-depracating comments
